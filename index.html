ABOUT

Welcome! My name is Carrie Gonzales, I’m an Author and Registered Dietitian and founder of CEG Wellness. I have over 15 years of clinical nutrition experience and more recently adding Integrative Nutrition Health Coach to my name. Like many women’s journey with PCOS, I was diagnosed in my early twenties and provided no education other than being handed a prescription for birth control and to come back to the doctor when I was ready to have kids.

I am one of the 1 in 10 women with PCOS who has spent the last 15 years wading through confusing PCOS related symptoms of, anxiety, depression, abnormal hormone levels, unexpected weight gain, body image and self-esteem loss. I have known what it is like to be your own personal guinea pig, experimenting with every diet under the sun. I’ve battled imposter syndrome, given my extensive nutrition background and not being able to “just figure things out”

Tired and frustrated to have all this nutrition knowledge but ending up in the same vicious PCOS cycle, I decided to challenge what I knew. I decided to question beyond what I was putting on my plate. I advanced my studies and now a certified health coach. Through a holistic approach I have managed to regulate my periods without prescription medication, improve my mood, battle cravings, and improve self-esteem and physique. Yes, healthy living with PCOS includes a solid nutritional foundation, but not without balancing out the other areas in your life.

What is PCOS?

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) is a hormonal disorder that affects 10-15% of women. PCOS can lead to complications including, infertility, obesity, hair and skin issues, anxiety, depression, endometrial cancer, type 2 diabetes as well as a host of other conditions. At this time, there is no cure for PCOS and working with a collaborative trusted team for PCOS management is ideal to help manage complications of this disorder.
